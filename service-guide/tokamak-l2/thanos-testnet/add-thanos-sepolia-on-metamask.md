# Add Thanos Sepolia on MetaMask





This section will guide you through the step-by-step process of installing the MetaMask wallet in your chrome browser. Additionally, we will delve into the procedure for adding the Thanos Sepolia network to the MetaMask wallet.

### How to Install MetaMask in Chrome

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 1.43.48 PM copy.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Open the Chrome browser and Login to Google ([https://www.google.com/](https://www.google.com/))
2. After login, click the **`⋮`** button on the top right as shown in the screenshot
3. Click on Extensions
4. Click on Visit Chrome Web Store



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 1.49.20 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After completing the above process, the Chrome Web Store window will open. In the Search bar on the screen, enter MetaMask



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 1.52.07 PM (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* When you search for MetaMask, you will see a screen resembling the one above. From there, click on the MetaMask extension to proceed.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 1.53.35 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* If you click MetaMask, the above screen will appear. Click **Add to Chrome** here.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 1.55.46 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* If the installation was successful, you should see that the **Add to Chrome** button has changed to **Remove from Chrome**, as shown above.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 1.43.48 PM.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. After installing MetaMask, click the **`⋮`** button on the top rignt, as shown in the screenshot&#x20;
2. Click Extensions&#x20;
3. Click Manage extensions



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 1.57.41 PM.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* You will enter the Manage Extensions window, as shown in the screenshot above. If it looks like the red box on the MetaMask side, the installation is complete.



### How to Add Thanos Sepolia Network in MetaMask (Method 1)

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.02.11 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* If you access [https://explorer.thanos-sepolia.tokamak.network/](https://explorer.thanos-sepolia.tokamak.network/), following screen will appear. Scroll down to the bottom.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/스크린샷 2024-08-26 오전 11.46.53.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Scroll down and you'll see the Add **Thanos Sepolia** button at the bottom with the Metamask icon as a red box. Click this button.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.06.12 PM.png" alt="" width="344"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Click the **Add Thanos Sepolia** button to open the MetaMask pop-up. Scroll all the way down to the bottom.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.08.14 PM.png" alt="" width="345"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* &#x20;Scroll down, you will see the **Approve** button as follows, press the **Approve** button.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.10.43 PM.png" alt="" width="358"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After you press the **Approve** button, you'll see the following screen. Click the **Switch Network** button.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/스크린샷 2024-08-26 오전 10.53.35.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After you press the **Switch Network** button and the network is successfully switched, you will see the Success pop-up shown above.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.14.47 PM.png" alt="" width="351"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* If you go back into the MetaMask again, you'll see that the network is displayed as Thanos Sepolia, as shown in the red section.

### How to Add Thanos Sepolia Network in MetaMask (method 2)

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.18.11 PM.png" alt="" width="351"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Click on the Metamask icon that you installed earlier, then click on the network button in red.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.19.49 PM.png" alt="" width="357"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After clicking, you'll see a screen that looks like this. Click the **Add Network** button at the bottom.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.21.50 PM.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After you click the **Add network** button, you'll see the following screen. On that screen, click **Add network manually** in red.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 2.23.20 PM.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* When you click the **Add network manually** button, you'll see the following screen.

> 1. Network Name: Thanos Sepolia
> 2. New RPC URL: [https://rpc.thanos-sepolia.tokamak.network](https://rpc.thanos-sepolia.tokamak.network)
> 3. Chain ID: 111551119090
> 4. Currency symbol: TON
> 5. Block explorer URL (optional): [https://explorer.thanos-sepolia.tokamak.network](https://explorer.thanos-sepolia.tokamak.network)

Fill in the fields with the above data and click **save** button.



<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/스크린샷 2024-08-26 오후 12.44.33.png" alt="" width="353"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* After saving, you can see the Thanos Sepolia network added, as shown above.
